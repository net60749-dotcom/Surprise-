Biology MCQs Interactive

Q1: What is the powerhouse of the cell?
Explanation: Mitochondria produce energy through cellular respiration.
Q2: Which macromolecule stores genetic information?
Explanation: DNA stores genetic information in cells.
Q3: What process do plants use to make food?
Explanation: Photosynthesis converts sunlight into chemical energy.
Q4: Which blood cells fight infections?
Explanation: White blood cells are key in immune defense.
Q5: What is the basic unit of life?
Explanation: The cell is the smallest unit that can carry out life functions.
